Welcome to a new look in XO Help 17.

If you are starting with an XO laptop you might jump straight to the top of the “Hardware section” Getting Started.

When you press the “next” link at the top right of this page you will move on to the page, The Sugar User Interface. To a new user this is a somethat theoretical page, but the following few pages covering navigation are more new user friendly and important for all new users.

The Activity you are using, Help, has its own Help page. Help will display, the pages in the index, plus (when you are online) pages linked to the internet.

In Help use the “Back” button to return to the previous page, and the “Home” button to return to Welcome to Sugar’s documentation!

Activities are listed in alphabetical order, starting with Browse.

The section of the table of Contents, Network, starts with the page Give Me the Internet

In Help pages, the green arrow is used to signify moving to material at a deeper level.
